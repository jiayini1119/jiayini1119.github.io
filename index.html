Jiayi (Jenny) Ni

I'm a Software Engineer at NVIDIA working on Agentic AI. I received my B.S. in Computer Science from the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA). I was honored to be advised by Professor Baharan Mirzasoleiman on data-efficient and robust representation learning, and by Professor Sriram Sankararaman on large-scale machine learning in genomics. I'm interested in making machine learning both efficient and robust across data, models, systems, and applications.
